About BookMood

Welcome to BookMood! I created this AI-powered book recommendation system to help readers discover their perfect next book based on their current emotional state. Whether you're feeling adventurous, melancholic, or seeking comfort, BookMood analyzes your mood and suggests books that resonate with your feelings.

This project combines my passion for literature, technology, and understanding human emotions. Using advanced AI algorithms, BookMood creates personalized reading experiences that go beyond traditional genre-based recommendations.
